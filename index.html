About Our School

Established in 1861, we(Z P Thangaon) have been a cornerstone of quality education in our community

Our Vision

>Our Vision at Z P Thangaon is to be a premier educational institution that nurtures confident, capable, and compassionate individuals ready to contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

Our Mission at Z P Thangaon is To provide inclusive, innovative, and quality education while fostering creativity, critical thinking, and character development through government-supported programs.

Key Achievements

  • Recognized as "Best Government School" in the district (2023)
  • 100% implementation of Digital India initiatives
  • 95% board examination pass rate for 5 consecutive years
  • State award for excellence in sports and cultural activities
  • First government school in region with solar power system

CEO's Message

"Education is the most powerful tool for transforming lives and communities. At our institution, we are committed to providing every student with opportunities to excel academically while developing strong moral values and leadership qualities."
- Mr Omkar Pawar
